## **Project Statement**

### **Problem Statement**
In today’s digital age, individuals have increased access to medical tests and health data such as blood reports and vital measurements. However, interpreting this data correctly often requires medical expertise. Many users struggle to understand what their values indicate, whether they fall within normal ranges, or if they suggest potential health risks.

This lack of clarity can lead to confusion, delayed action, or unnecessary anxiety. There is a clear need for a system that can bridge the gap between raw medical data and meaningful interpretation by presenting results in a simplified and understandable manner.

BloodIQ addresses this issue by providing a platform that accepts basic health inputs and translates them into clear insights, helping users become more aware of their health status.

---

### **Scope of the Project**
The scope of this project is limited to developing a web-based application that performs basic health risk analysis using predefined logic and scoring methods. The system focuses on three key areas:

- **Diabetes Risk Assessment** using parameters such as glucose level, BMI, age, and related factors  
- **CBC Analysis** to identify abnormal blood parameters like RBC, WBC, hemoglobin, platelets, and MCV  
- **Blood Pressure Prediction** based on lifestyle inputs such as stress, sleep, exercise, and physical measurements  

The application is designed for educational and preliminary analysis purposes only. It does not aim to replace medical diagnosis or provide clinical accuracy.

---

### **Target Users**
The application is intended for a broad range of users, including:

- Individuals who want quick and basic insights into their health  
- Users who have access to medical reports but lack the knowledge to interpret them  
- Students and beginners exploring healthcare-related applications or data analysis  
- Anyone interested in understanding how health data can be processed and visualized in a web application  

---

### **High-Level Features**
- **Multi-module health analysis system** covering diabetes, CBC, and blood pressure  
- **User input interface** for entering medical and lifestyle data  
- **Rule-based processing engine** to evaluate inputs and generate results  
- **Risk classification system** (Low, Moderate, High) for easy understanding  
- **Detection of abnormal values** in CBC reports with possible interpretations  
- **Estimated blood pressure calculation** with categorized output  
- **Instant result display** without page reload using asynchronous requests  
- **Simple and responsive UI** designed for clarity and ease of use  
- **Structured output format** including risk level, score, detected issues, and advice
